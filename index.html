TwinsAtPlay

Connecting Children Through Play

Our Mission

TwinsAtPlay is a unique platform where children with autism and their peers come together to play, learn, and grow. We facilitate meaningful connections through carefully curated playdates, fostering understanding and friendship.

  • Connect: Bringing families together
  • Play: Fun activities for all children
  • Grow: Building lasting friendships

Meet Saranya

Hi, I'm Saranya Senthilkumar, founder of TwinsAtPlay. My journey began with my twin sister Pavi, who has autism. Watching her grow, I realized the profound impact of friendship and understanding on a child's development.

Why We Started TwinsAtPlay:

  • Create a safe, inclusive space for all children
  • Connect families for meaningful playdates
  • Promote empathy and awareness in our community
  • Ensure every child experiences the joy of friendship

Through my experiences with Pavi, I saw firsthand the challenges children with autism face in social situations. I also witnessed the beautiful moments of connection when understanding and acceptance were present. This inspired me to create TwinsAtPlay, a platform that bridges the gap and creates opportunities for all children to play, learn, and grow together.

How It Works

  1. Sign Up: Parents of children with autism and families interested in inclusive playdates can join our community.
  2. Connect: Our platform helps you find compatible families in your area.
  3. Schedule: Arrange playdates at times that work for everyone.
  4. Play: Enjoy fun, inclusive activities that all children will love.
  5. Grow: Watch as friendships blossom and children develop social skills.

We carefully design each playdate to ensure a positive experience for all children involved. Our team of experts creates activities that are engaging, educational, and adaptable to different needs and abilities.
